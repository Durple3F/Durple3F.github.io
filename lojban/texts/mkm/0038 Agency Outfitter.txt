When Agency Outfitter enters the battlefield, you may search your graveyard, hand, and/or library for a card named Magnifying Glass and/or a card named Thinking Cap and put them onto the battlefield. If you search your library this way, shuffle.
After each time this enters the battlefield, you can search for a card for which "grow-see lens" is the name of it, and/or a card for which "thought hat" is the name of it, from among the set of cards which are in your library, or your graveyard, or your hand, and put them into the battlefield. If you searched at your library, you shuffle.
