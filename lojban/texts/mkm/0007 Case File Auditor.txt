When Case File Auditor enters the battlefield and whenever you solve a Case, look at the top six cards of your library. You may reveal an enchantment card from among them and put it into your hand. Put the rest on the bottom of your library in a random order.
After each time this enters the battlefield, and each time you solve a mystery, you look at the set of 6 cards which are atop your library. You can have yourself reveal 1 of those cards which is an enchantment and put it into the battlefield. You put each of those cards which remain into the bottom of your library, ordered by randomness.
