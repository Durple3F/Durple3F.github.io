Spheal is a chubby, spherical, seal-like Pokémon with a short, flat tail. Its plushy fur is light blue with several white spots of various sizes on its back. This covering of fluffy fur, combined with the layer of blubber underneath, retains heat and protects Spheal from the cold. It has black, circular eyes; small tusks protruding from its upper jaw; and small, rounded ears. Its stubby flippers and its underside are both beige.
The Sfokia is a chubby, spherical Pokémon that looks like with seal, with a short, flat tail. The otter-like fur of it is light blue with several white spots of different sizes on the back of it. This covering of fluffy fur, together with the layer of fat from below, retains the heat and protects the Sfokia from the cold. It has black, circular eyes; small tusks which protrude from the upper jaw; and small, round ears. The short, chubby fins of it and the under part of it are beige.

Η Σφώκια είναι ένα παχουλό, σφαιρικό Ποκέμον που μοιάζει με φώκια, με κοντή, επίπεδη ουρά. Η λούτρινη γούνα της είναι γαλάζια με αρκετές άσπρες κηλίδες διαφόρων μεγεθών στην πλάτη της. Αυτό το κάλυμμα της αφράτης γούνας, μαζί με το στρώμα τους λίπους από κάτω, διατηρεί τη θερμότητα και προστατεύει τη Σφώκια από το κρύο. Έχει μαύρα, κύκλια μάτια· μικρούς χαυλιόδοντες που προεξέχουν από το πάνω σαγόνι· και μικρά, στρογγυλά αυτιά. Τα κοντόχοντρα πτερύγια της και το κάτω μέρος της είναι μπεζ.
When it is happy or cheerful, such as when eating in a group, this Pokémon will clap its fins. The mealtimes of Spheal have been described as noisy as a result. Because its body is poorly shaped for swimming and can only walk slowly, it moves primarily by rolling across ice floes. This Pokémon lives in and around the sea. Spheal also lives on any drift ice that comes near the shore in order to avoid predators. Spheal are often juggled by its evolved form Sealeo, which does so to check the former's health. When a Spheal finds prey while drifting at sea, it will inform the Walrein leader of its herd on where to find the prey.
When it is happy or cheerful, such as when it eats in a group, this Pokémon will clap the fins of it. The times of meals of Sfokia have been described as noisy as a result. Because the body of it is badly formed for swimming and it can only walk slowly, it moves mainly with rolling atop of the icebergs. This Pokémon lives in and near to the sea. The Sfokia lives also atop of whatever drifting ice that approaches the shore for avoiding predators. The Sfokias often are tossed playfully by the evolved form of them, Leokia, who does this to check the health of the Sfokia. When a Sfokia finds prey, it will inform the Vasillipo leader of the herd of it for where it is found.
